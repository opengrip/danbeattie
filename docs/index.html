Bio

Dan Beattie is the Music Director and bassist for The Martin Boys, the fast-rising country duo currently crossing North America on tour in support of their debut album. Along the way the band has picked up national television attention and a fanbase that grows with every city.

Before The Martin Boys, Dan was the bassist for Eleven Past One, a Canadian pop-rock band whose single "The World Is Ours" charted nationally and earned them radio play across the country. That chapter included national tours and a deep education in what it takes to build a show from the ground up.

With over 20 years of experience, Dan brings a seasoned approach to every stage he plays. From arena-sized festival bills to intimate club rooms, the work is the same — hold down the pocket and make the band sound bigger than it is.

On tour, summer '26.
